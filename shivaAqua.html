About Shiva

Meet Shiva: Your Trusted Aqua Partner

With over 15 years of experience in the aquaculture industry, Shiva has established himself as a leading expert in hatchery management and seafood supply chain from the coastal region of Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh.

His deep understanding of both seed production and seafood export markets makes him a unique bridge between hatcheries, aquaculture farmers, and global buyers.

Shiva manages a network of premium hatcheries producing high-quality seeds while also facilitating direct exports of fresh seafood from local farmers to restaurants and companies worldwide.

Based in Kakinada, Coastal Andhra Pradesh

Our Services

Hatchery Management

Professional management and quality control for shrimp, fish, and crab hatcheries to ensure premium seed production.

  • SPF (Specific Pathogen Free) certification
  • Advanced breeding techniques
  • Water quality management
  • Disease prevention protocols

Seed Supply Network

Reliable supply of high-quality aquaculture seeds to farmers across Andhra Pradesh and neighboring states.

  • Tiger Prawn seeds (PL-10 to PL-12)
  • Crab seedlings
  • Fish fingerlings (Rohu, Catla, Mrigal)
  • Seasonal availability planning

Seafood Export & Import

Direct sourcing from aquaculture farmers to international buyers, restaurants, and seafood companies.

  • Fresh & frozen seafood exports
  • Quality certification & packaging
  • Logistics & cold chain management
  • Market linkage for farmers
